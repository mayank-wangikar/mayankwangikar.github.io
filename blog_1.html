So I started a blog, this is where y'all would find material to make me cringe and embarrass me in public. I mean it is mostly going to be rambling about random things, I learned, found out, or happened to me in a given week. I am not a good writer by any stretch so, don't expect me to give any deep insight. Expect more of "Why lift yourself is the best Kanye song", "All major world leaders are incels" or "Government is probably spying on me, but I don't care."
